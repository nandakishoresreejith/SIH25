The Water Crisis Challenge

Every year, billions of liters of rainwater go to waste while communities face water scarcity. We're here to change that.

The Problem

Urban areas waste 60-80% of rainwater through runoff, while groundwater levels continue to deplete at alarming rates.

Our Solution

Smart rooftop rainwater harvesting systems that capture, filter, and channel water directly into groundwater aquifers.

The Impact

Increase groundwater levels, reduce urban flooding, and ensure sustainable water supply for future generations.
